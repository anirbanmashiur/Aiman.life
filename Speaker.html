Take a Data-Centric Approach to Manage Your Data and Minimize Risk

Qualifying and quantifying risks of an organization are the quintessential job description of any risk manager. But how can these professionals assess new markets for risks, emerging risks in current markets or when to take risks? Making an enterprise data-centric risk strategy is the key. In this session, risk managers will learn from data industry expert Aiman El-Ramly on how to identify from data risks, opportunities and quantify an organization’s ability to mitigate or seize these. Finally, how can an organization take this established framework and provide an enterprise data-centric risk evaluation platform based upon innumerable data sources?

Learning Objectives:

  • Risk professionals will learn about new and emerging risks derived from Data and Analytics

  • Risk professionals will learn what data-centric processes are required for qualifying and identifying such risks.

  • Quantification of risk through external and internal data analysis.

  • How to gain transparent and auditable enterprise Data Governance to assess risks for organizations.

How to Implement the Right Data Architecture to Support Machine Learning

Developing practical data strategies to support Energy Business use cases.

With the proliferation of data and the need to extract intelligence to support critical business operations, companies are struggling to seek the right architecture for data analytics and machine learning. Aiman provides examples of how to explore and implement advanced data and analytic solutions.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understanding the fundamentals of big data, data management, analytics, machine learning, and cloud computing

  • Developing business viable data and analytics strategies

  • Tips for a successful data-driven organization

  • How to design and implement practical advanced data analytics and machine learning
